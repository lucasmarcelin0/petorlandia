## 🎯 Problem Identified

### 7 Critical Integration Gaps

```
Gap #1: Consulta → Accounting Link Missing
   └─ Impact: Revenue leakage if services not formally quoted

Gap #2: Payment → Vendor Payment Reconciliation Missing  
   └─ Impact: Impossible to match customer vs vendor cash flows

Gap #3: Service → Invoice Item Reconciliation Missing
   └─ Impact: Unbilled work goes untracked

Gap #4: Insurance Coverage → Accounting Separation Missing
   └─ Impact: Cannot isolate insurance vs private pay

Gap #5: PlantonistaEscala → PJPayment Circular Dependencies
   └─ Impact: Payment status conflicts, payroll errors

Gap #6: ClinicTaxes Dynamic Recalculation Missing
   └─ Impact: Tax calculations stale 1-2 weeks into month

Gap #7: BlocoOrcamento Discount Tracking Missing
   └─ Impact: Financial reports don't match actual collections
```
